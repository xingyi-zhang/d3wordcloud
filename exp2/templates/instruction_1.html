For each trial, you will be presented with first a cross (+). Fixate on the cross and then either a word cloud like the one below or a single word. If time allows, silently read the center word to yourself. You will only be shown the word or wordcloud for a limited time and the time will vary. Then you will be shown a target in capital letters. The target is either a common English word or a nonword. Your task is to press right arrow key or F key if the target is an English word, and press the left arrow key or J key if the target is not an English word. You should press the key as quickly and as accurately as possible.

For instance, after the word cloud below is presented, it will disappear and the target "nitable" will be shown. Since "nitable" is not an English word, pressing the left arrow key or F key would be the correct answer.

After your response is registered, you may press the space bar to proceed to the next trial, at your pace. There will be 90 trials with a break in the middle. However, if you need to stop at any time, just don't press the space bar after you have responded.
